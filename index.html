About us

Nowadays, there are many website builders that are aimed at creators. Especially for TikTok and Instagram, you can find a lot of simple link-in-bio style websites. Our "Biory" is a pre-release website builder that enhances simple link-in-bio style websites and allows you to decorate multiple social media referral pages to your liking in a matter of minutes. From now on, our goal is to provide creators with a set of tools that will allow them to build various revenue streams through communication, such as the ability to link to their original products, paid content sales sites, and donation sites.
